Stat Names

Starship modifiers that improve abilities ("Missile Battery — Threat +2"). One stat per lane; Firepower, Armor, Warp, and Energy sit beneath the lanes — the bench — and hold unless a row says otherwise. The ion stat reads engineering — the state of your engine room — not movement; the movement pool died with Mobility. Threat is accepted for now. Rows reuse words freely — they're competing readings of the same slots, not rivals for different ones.

—Protonreal, observed — offense but ammo-coded; loses to Plasma.
—Electronreal, observed — mundane; this is "power," which Reactor owns.
—Positronreal, observed — AI/computer-coded. Module word, not a resource.
—Neutronreal, observed — warhead/star flavor word, no lane.
—Neutrinoreal, observed — through-matter detection; sensor-coded but faint.

—Antimatterreal, lab-produced — cores and warheads; Reactor vocabulary, not a lane.
